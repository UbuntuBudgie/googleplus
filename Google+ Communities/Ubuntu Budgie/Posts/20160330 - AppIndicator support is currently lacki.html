AppIndicator support is currently lacking in budgie-remix/budgie-desktop.  We need someone with a little bit of C knowledge to write a wrapper around one of the existing panel indicator implementations.  Please put your handup if you can help with this.

Until then - just going to share this with you (look bottom right):

    sudo apt install xfce4-panel xfce4-indicator-plugin

Hint - move the budgie panel to the bottom before launching xfce4-panel

Right click xfce4-panel and remove everything - and then add the indicator plugin.  Unlock the panel and move it away from the top.  Change budgie-panel back to the top.  Move the xfce4 panel to the bottom and lock it.  Make it transparent, and make it intelli-hide. Voila!

Don't forget to launch xfce4-panel from your startup-applications.
