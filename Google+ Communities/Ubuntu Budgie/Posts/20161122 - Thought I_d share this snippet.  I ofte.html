Thought I'd share this snippet.  I often find that when printing stuff I've forgotten to turn the printer on - I'm often left scratching my head wondering why nothing has appeared - the odd notification is not a good enough clue - I want direct access to the print queue ... through an indicator or applet that is visible when  print jobs are just being queued.

Use Menu - Startup Applications and use the command system-config-printer-applet

logout and login - next time you see that icon appear - click to investigate what is up with your print job.
